Route 01
By train from London
Direct services from London St Pancras reach Bedford Midland in 1 hour 11 minutes. East Midlands Railway runs frequent services all day. Taxis serve the park directly from the station.

Questions answered

Everything people ask about Universal Bedford

Updated every time new information is officially confirmed.

When does Universal Studios Bedford open?+
Universal Studios Bedford is targeting a 2031 opening. Construction officially began in January 2026 following government planning approval in December 2025. We update this page the moment any official date is confirmed — sign up to our newsletter to be first to know.
Where exactly is Universal Studios Bedford located?+
The park is being built on a 476-acre site near Kempston Hardwick, approximately 3 miles south of Bedford town centre. The site is bordered by the A421 to the west and the Midland Main Line to the east. Two new railway stations are planned as part of the wider development.
How do I get to Universal Studios Bedford from London?+
Bedford is approximately 1 hour 11 minutes from London St Pancras by direct train on East Midlands Railway. From Bedford Midland station, the park is 3 miles south by taxi. Luton Airport, 30 minutes away, will be the main arrival point for international visitors.
What hotels are nearest to Universal Studios Bedford?+
The closest hotels are currently in Bedford town centre, around 3 miles from the site — including Holiday Inn Express Bedford, Mercure Bedford Centre, Premier Inn Bedford South and The Bedford Swan Hotel. New hotels closer to the site are expected to open from 2028 onwards.
What rides will Universal Studios Bedford have?+
Universal has not yet confirmed specific attractions. Based on industry analysis, likely franchises include Jurassic World, Minions, Nintendo World and potentially UK-specific IP. Our news section tracks every rumour and official statement as it emerges.
When will Universal Bedford tickets go on sale?+
Tickets are not yet on sale. Based on how Universal has launched previous parks globally, tickets typically go on sale 6–12 months before opening — suggesting around late 2030. Sign up to our newsletter to be the first to know when they become available.
